Sales and Order Management

To record what customers want and move it through quotes, orders, delivery, invoicing, payment and follow-up. This page presents the section logic: the user task flow first, then the task-based TOC that follows that flow.

🔍  Search, for example "create a sales order"
Presentation noteThis page shows why the Sales TOC is arranged this way. It is not replacing F1 mapping; it gives users a workflow entry into the same help topics.

Why this section exists

Sales users usually think in terms of the work they are trying to finish: quote, order, pick, deliver, invoice, receive payment, or review what is outstanding. The TOC is therefore arranged by the normal order of sales work rather than by ribbon position only.

Who comes here

Sales admin, customer service, warehouse/dispatch, accounts receivable and managers reviewing sales activity. Existing users can still use F1 or the Ribbon and Menu reference when they know the exact screen.

User task flow behind this TOC
Set up sales→Quote and order→Pick, pack and deliver→Invoice customers→Receive payments→Follow up / review
Selected task-based TOC group

Quote and order

Purpose and result

Use this group when creating, changing or reviewing customer quotes and sales orders. The output is a saved or processed quote/order that can move to picking, delivery, invoicing or review.

F1 compatibility: users can still press F1 from the Sales Quote or Sales Order screen to reach the mapped topic. This TOC gives another route into the same content.
