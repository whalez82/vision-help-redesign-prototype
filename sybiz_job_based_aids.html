Job-Based Aids

Role shortcuts to workflow topics

Job-Based Aids are a presentation concept for users who think by responsibility rather than module. They do not replace the workflow TOC; they link role-based needs back to the same topics.

Design intent: “I am a Sales Admin — what do I need today?” becomes a valid way to enter the manual.
Role aid

Sales Admin

Common reasons: create a quote, turn it into an order, amend an order, invoice a customer, review outstanding transactions.

Quote→Order→Pick / Deliver→Invoice→Review
